About

past

I started my studies with a Business Administration Degree. At the time, I had a job in the Financial Industry and decided on a Master in Finance. Even though it went very well, I wasn’t passionate about my work and I felt like something was missing. I decided it was time for a career change, and began to discover the Creative Industry.

present

On The Next Web blog, I found an article about Codaisseur, a startup that offers coding bootcamps. At that time, I was following a few coding courses online. Because I wanted to learn more in a real life experience, I applied immediately. So here I am.  

The course is really intense, but I’ve learned a lot. The stack includes JavaScript, jQuery, ReactJS, Ruby, Rails and more. Also, I learned to use Github, and the command line. Each week I worked in teams on diverse projects, like a Slackbot or Chat App.

future

Now, I’m looking for an awesome Front-End developer job, with nice colleagues. I will keep on designing and coding, it’s my goal to become a Full Stack developer with time. I like many other things, like Marketing, Design Thinking, User Experience Design, Illustration and Animation to name a few. I can’t wait to join a professional team, and create amazing experiences.
